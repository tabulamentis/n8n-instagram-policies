Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Information We Access

Our application accesses the following information from your Instagram account:

  • Basic profile information (username, profile picture)
  • Media content (photos, videos, captions)
  • Media metadata (timestamps, engagement metrics)
  • Comments and messages (if applicable permissions are granted)

2. How We Use Your Information

The information accessed is used exclusively for:

  • Automated workflow processing through n8n
  • Content management and organization
  • Analytics and reporting automation
  • Integration with other business tools and platforms

3. Data Storage and Security

We are committed to protecting your data:

  • Data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored unless specifically configured
  • All data transmission occurs over secure HTTPS connections
  • Access tokens are stored securely and encrypted
  • We follow industry-standard security practices

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your Instagram data with third parties.

Your data may only be processed through the automated workflows you configure in your n8n instance.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access permissions at any time through your Instagram settings
  • Request information about what data we access
  • Request deletion of any stored data
  • Modify the scope of permissions granted

6. Data Retention

We retain access to your Instagram data only as long as:

  • You maintain an active connection to our application
  • Your access tokens remain valid
  • You do not revoke permissions

Data processing stops immediately when access is revoked.

7. Compliance

This application complies with:

  • Instagram Basic Display API Terms of Use
  • Meta Platform Terms of Service
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable
